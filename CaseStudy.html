Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is recipe for deliciously rich Scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the pan to the centre every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
